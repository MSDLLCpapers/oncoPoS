# Transform degree of heterogeneity to denominator

Helper function: transform degree of heterogeneity to denominator

## Usage

``` r
get_denominator(input)
```

## Arguments

- input:

  indicates the degree of heterogeneity of the study level parameter,
  and must be one of "large", "substantial", "moderate", "small", "very
  small"
